    Convector Heaters

    Luxurious warm air circulation

    Convection heaters gently circulate currents of warm air, maintaining blissful heat and radiating it around your home for a rapid heat solution and ultimate comfort.

  • Continuous circulation of warm air currents allows for rapid heat distribution throughout small to medium rooms.

  • Compact, portable design means ultimate transportability between rooms to customise your room temperature.

  • Silent operation provides ultimate peace without interference or disruption.

    Fan Heaters

    Targeted Heat Flow Solution

    Passing through the internal blades of the fan, powerful warm air is released for a rapid, targeted heat flow and optimum warmth.

  • Versatile solution to heating small to medium spaces with easy to use functionality.

  • Direct heat flow for instant warmth with adjustable thermostat.

  • Overprotection and tip-over switch provides optimum safety.

  • Rapid heat solution without the hassle or waiting time of conventional heating methods.

  • Fully adjustable settings allow for customised temperatures throughout your home.

  • Energy efficient providing unparalleled performance at the flick of a switch, providing heat as and when you need it.
